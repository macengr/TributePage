Chronology

Return to top
  • 1927: Born in Erie, Pennsylvania
  • 1944: Enlisted in the Army Air Corps, a junior in high school
  • 1951: Enlists in U.S. Air Force
  • 1953: F86 pilot in Korea
  • 1975: Retires from Air Force
  • 1997: Died, buried in Arlington Cemetery with full military honors
